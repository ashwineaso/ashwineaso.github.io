The Design

Learners have the freedom to choose what they want to cook and eat. They can personalize their learning based on what matters to them.

The recipe is broken into smaller chunks of information to enable the learner to get a quick mental picture of the entire process, yet go through the recipe focusing on one step at a time.

Once the video for a step is finished, the learner confirms that they have completed it. The app adapts to what they have learned and does not show the tutorial for this step in the next recipe unless they explicitly ask for it.

Process

Concept sketches

We were all passionate about food, so as a team, we chose the idea of making an app to teach cooking. I printed mobile wireframe templates and led a brainstorming session to sketch ideas collaboratively. To get inspiration, we looked at other food apps including Yummly, Food Network.

Wireframes

I detailed the ideas from our brainstorming session as wireframes. I worked with another team member to connect app features to the learning theories taught in class.
